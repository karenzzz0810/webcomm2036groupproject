Transmission of coronavirus via mosquito bites

Although it’s always appropriate to keep a safe distance from the insect that spreads paludism and dengue fever, respiratory viruses are not transmitted by mosquito bites, but by droplets of saliva or nasal secretions expelled by an infected person when coughing or sneezing.


Lockdown

 There is rumours saying that government may impose a “lockdown” in view of the covid outbreak. Many citizens stocked up on frozen food and tins to in case of a sudden lockdown and the shelves in supermarkets became nearly empty. However, that is fake news and Hong Kong government clarified that there is no plan to lockdown the city.
